Hospitality

The Woven Terrace

Client

Ramaya INN Hotel

Location

Bhupatwala, Haridwar, Uttarakhand

Area

2,000 Sq. Ft.

Role

Interior Designing

Texture, Pattern, and the Joy of Gathering

The Woven Terrace is a 2,000 sq.ft rooftop hospitality interior at Ramaya INN Hotel in Bhupatwala, Haridwar — a space designed as a celebration of texture, pattern, and the pleasure of outdoor gathering. The design weaves together materials, light, and landscape into a rich tapestry.

Rattan, woven fabrics, terracotta planters, and carefully positioned lighting create an environment that feels simultaneously handcrafted and architecturally precise. The Woven Terrace is a space that rewards the senses — a place where the distinction between indoors and outdoors dissolves into the pleasure of the moment.
