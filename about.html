Ar. Kartik & I.D. Akshay

Lines & Thoughts is a design studio started in 2020 by Ar. Kartik Khurana and I.D. Akshay Khurana. We work on architecture, interiors, and master planning — turning every project into a space that feels personal and meaningful.

We believe every space has its own story. Our process starts with understanding what the client needs, then shapes it around the place, climate, materials, and how people will actually live or work in that space. We focus on clean design, good detailing, and spaces that feel as good as they look.

Our work includes luxury homes, hotels, commercial spaces, and custom interiors. No matter the project size, we focus on quality, creativity, and design that lasts. We also keep sustainability in mind — using natural light, smart material choices, and climate-friendly planning wherever we can.

We work closely with clients, consultants, and craftsmen to bring every idea to life with care and precision.

Lines & Thoughts isn't just a design studio — it's a way of thinking. We create spaces that people don't just see, but feel.
