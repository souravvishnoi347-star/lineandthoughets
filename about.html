"Designing spaces that balance emotion, function, and timeless beauty — made to be experienced, remembered, and lived."

Lines & Thoughts is a design studio founded in 2020 by Ar. Kartik Khurana and I.D. Akshay Khurana, working across architecture, interiors, and master planning. We believe every space has a story — so we design around the client's needs, the place, and how people actually live in it.

From luxury homes to hotels and commercial spaces, our focus stays the same: thoughtful design, good craftsmanship, and spaces that last — built with sustainability and natural light in mind.

We work closely with clients and craftsmen to bring every idea to life, the right way.
